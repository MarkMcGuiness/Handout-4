Dead Larry's First Full length album, released in 2008. The Intro track tells the origin of the bands name and 12 more tracks follow and tell stories of real experiernces from the band members. The album included a variety of guest artists.
Story Time
…as the radio...
Dead Larry’s second full length album, and first official concept album. Recorded in 2011, The album mimics tuning through a radio, including fictional commercial, radio shows, and news stories between the 13 songs. The album included a variety of guest artists, as well a local Iowa radio host
Mona-Lisa Stellarum
Dead Larry’s third full length album and second concept album. Released in 2015 with a whopping 20 tracks, this album is a science fiction rock opera of sorts, complete with narration and sound effects between tracks to tell the full story of the four band members imagined as characters in an adventure through time and space.
Never Made it to Space
Dead Larry’s second EP with just 5 songs.Released in 2013. The Disc itself also contained Dead Larry’s first two music videos, including a stop animation video for the title track that brought the album art to life.
